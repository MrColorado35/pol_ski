Practice

Why Regular Practice Matters

Studies show that spaced repetition - reviewing words at increasing intervals - is one of the most effective ways to move vocabulary from short-term to long-term memory.

Our practice system uses this approach to help you retain Polish words with minimal effort. Just spend 5-10 minutes per day practicing, and you'll be surprised how quickly you build a permanent Polish vocabulary!

Tip: For best results, practice a little each day rather than a lot once per week.
